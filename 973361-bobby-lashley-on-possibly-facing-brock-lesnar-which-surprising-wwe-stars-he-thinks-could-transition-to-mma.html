Bobby Lashley On Possibly Facing Brock Lesnar, Which Surprising WWE Stars He Thinks Could Excel In M

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  220 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Bobby Lashley recently spoke with James Stewart for 98.5 The Sports Hub in Boston while promoting tonight’s RAW taping; you can read a few transcribed highlights below:

(Transcription credit to Bill Pritchard for Wrestlezone.com) 

That is definitely something I would be interested in. Ever since I’ve started in the business everyone has compared Brock [Lesnar] and I, so I think that’s a match that just needs to happen at some point in time.

I think everybody has an opportunity—of course if you had a background—an athletic background or rather a combat background that would give you a better opportunity. But most of the guys currently in MMA don’t have a background in wrestling. If you’re talking about somebody that could just hop right into it, of course you’d have to bring up Chad Gable. He was an Olympian, I believe that he could go in there and he has the work ethic; he’s an animal in the ring working out. The Authors Of Pain—both of those guys—[Gzim Selmani aka ‘Rezar] is a kickboxing world champion. [Sunny Dhinsa aka Akam] is a multi-time wrestling champion. So both of those guys, and [Rezar] fought for Bellator for a while, so he could do it.
