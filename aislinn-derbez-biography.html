Who is Aislinn Derbez?

Aislinn Derbez is a Mexican actress and model. In 2018, she started appearing in the Netflix series ‘La Casa de las Flores’ earning her international fame.

Aislin Derbez was born Aislinn González Michel on 18 March 1987, in Mexico City, Mexico.

As of 2023, her age is 36. She was born to parents Eugenio Derbez (father) and Gabriela Michel (mother).

Aislinn Derbez: Professional Life, Career

Aislinn Derbez started her career as a model when she was just 15 years old. She also landed several small roles in movies and acted in the famous play, ‘Happy Hour’.

Derbez made her debut with the short film, ‘Served Cold’ (2008) as Mercedes.

In 2010, she appeared in two movies, ‘El atentado’ and ‘YTe presento a Laura’. She appeared in Sobre Ella and Little Baby Jesus in 2013.

In the year 2016, she added two more acting credits to her name with Comprades and Macho as Maria and Viviana respectively. Similarly, in 2019, she portrayed Isabel in ‘Miss Bala’.

Derbez also has several credits in Television. She made her TV debut as Vanessa in ‘Ellas son, la alegria del hogar’. She appeared as Brenda in six episodes of Nina’s mal.

A couple of years later, she appeared in six episodes of Los heroes del Norte. Similarly, in 2013, she appeared as Giovanna in Gossip Girl: Acapulco.

Since 2018, she has been portraying the leading role of Elena de la Mora in the Netflix series, ‘La casa de las Flores’.
