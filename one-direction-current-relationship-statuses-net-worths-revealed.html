One Direction: Current Relationship Statuses And Net Worths, Revealed

 Posted on July 20, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  981 words  |  Aldo Pusey

One Direction is a pop boyband that released music from 2010-2016. They got together on The X-Factor in England, and while they didn’t win, they took over the world with their music. Over the years, the boys continued to grow and change, as is evidenced by every new album one of them releases.

Since their “indefinite hiatus,” Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Zayn Malik, Louis Tomlinson, and Harry Styles have continued to further their careers in music and entertainment. Each band member has released solo albums and singles and continued to go on individual tours. They have also been romantically linked to numerous women over the years. Here are their current relationship statuses and net worths.

10 Niall Horan Is Dating Fashionista Amelia Woolley

In the summer of 2020, Niall Horan announced to the world via social media that he had a girlfriend. Her name is Amelia Woolley, a fashion buyer/fashionista, and they are reportedly happier than ever. The two have taken serious steps in their relationship, like going on vacations and spending holidays together, especially as Niall took Amelia home to Ireland this past winter to celebrate Christmas in his hometown.

9 Niall Horan's Net Worth Is Currently At $70 Million

After the break of One Direction, Niall Horan continued his singing and songwriting career. So far, he has released two major albums: Flicker in 2017 and HeartbreakWeather in 2020, as well as an EP last year called “Niall’s Mix.” Between his own world tours and traveling with other musicians, Horan has consistently been on the road from the time of the split until 2019, helping to boost his net worth to $70 million.

8 Liam Payne Is Engaged Again To His Former Fiancée Maya Henry

Maya Henry is a professional model—both on magazine covers and the runway. She and Liam Payne were officially photographed out and about together in 2019, and their relationship led Payne to propose in August 2020. Less than a year later, during the summer of last year, Liam decided to break off their engagement. The pair got back together later last year, Maya making it Instagram official in September.

7 Liam Payne Currently Has A $60 Million Net Worth

Liam Payne has continued to sing and write songs since the end of his boyband career. He has released two albums and one EP thus far, “First Time” in 2018, LP1 in 2019, and Midnight Hour in 2020. He is still coming up with new songs to release and is teaming up with other singers to make music. Thanks to his music, he has raised his net worth to $60 million.

6 Zayn Malik Is No Longer Dating His Baby Mama Gigi Hadid

Zayn Malik recently broke up with his long-term girlfriend, Gigi Hadid. A famous supermodel, Hadid has been in the model industry for years, and she officially started dating Zayn in 2015. They took a break from 2018-2020, but when they got back together their romance was aflame, as the two announced Gigi’s pregnancy in April 2020. They have continued to be on-again/off-again, but he is still involved in Hadid’s life.

5 Zayn Malik's Net Worth Currently Rests At $65 Million

The first to leave the One Direction group, Zayn Malik has brought his net worth up to $65 million. He has released four albums since the split: Mind of Mine in 2016, Icarus Falls in 2018, GoldPharoah in 2020, and NobodyIs Listening came out last year. He has also been hired to release over a dozen singles, not only for his own label, but for other companies as well.

4 There Are Unconfirmed Rumors Louis Tomlinson And Eleanor Calder Are Still Together

During his time with One Direction, Louis Tomlinson was dating his English model/fashionista girlfriend Eleanor Calder. Tomlinson is usually very under-wraps with his relationships, so the exact time they got together and broke up is unknown, but from 2015-2017 he was seeing two other women. He got one of them pregnant, and he and Briana Jungwirth welcomed a son in 2016. It has been reported, however, that he has gotten back with Calder since then.

3 Louis Tomlinson Has A Current Net Worth Of $70 Million

Since walking away from One Direction (a decision that really gutted Tomlinson), Louis has continued to make and release music. He has only made one album thus far, Walls in 2020, but has written numerous singles. With all his music, he has been able to tour the world, performing sold out shows. This success has brought his net worth to $70 million.

2 Harry Styles Has Been Dating Olivia Wilde For Over A Year

Harry Styles and Olivia Wilde have been dating for over a year after developing a relationship through working on Wilde’s movie Don’t Worry, Darling starting in 2020. Styles was cast in the film, and the two were spotted out together in January 2021. In the last year, the two have continued to date and grow closer together as Harry has been slowly getting to know Olivia’s kids.

1 Harry Styles Currently Has An $80 Million Net Worth

Over the years of his solo career, Harry Styles has continued to branch out and release music. He has currently released two albums in 2017 and 2019, Harry Styles and Fine Line respectively. Styles has also dipped his toe into acting, not only with Olivia Wilde, but in the MCU via the end credits scene of Eternals, hinting at his joining the cinematic universe. He has the highest net worth of all the guys with $80 million.
