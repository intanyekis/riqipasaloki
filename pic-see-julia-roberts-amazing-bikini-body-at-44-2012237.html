Oscar-winning actress Julia Roberts flaunted her stunning bikini bod on Thursday while vacationing with her family.

Roberts, 44, wore green halter top two-piece swimsuit while hanging out poolside at her Hawaii hotel. The flattering bikini showed off the star's flat tummy and long, lean legs. See more photos of Julia Roberts' family vacation.

But the Mirror, Mirror actress wasn't just lounging around working on her tan. The hands-on mom showed off her famous grin while playing her kids — twins Hazel and Phinnaeus, 7, and Henry, 5 — in the pool. Hawaii seems to be a favorite vacation spot for Roberts and her husband Danny Moder, 43, as they frequently vacation there.
