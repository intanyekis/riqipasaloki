Keiko Agena is an American actress who is well-known for portraying Lane Kim on the popular television series Gilmore Girls and NYPD medical examiner Dr. Edrisa Tanaka on the FOX crime drama Prodigal Son.

What is the Net Worth of Keiko Agena? Salary, Earnings

The actress has accepted 70 acting credits since 1993. The estimated net worth of Keiko Agena is $300,000, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Where was Keiko Agena born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Christine Keiko Agena is the complete name of Keiko Agena. She was born in Honolulu, Hawaii, on October 3, 1973. Her mother is Claire Agena, while her father’s identity is unknown.

Keiko attended the preparatory school at the Mid-Pacific Institute. Similarly, she enlisted at Whitman College for a year as a drama major.

Agena possesses Japanese-American citizenship and is of blended ancestry.

Is Keiko Agena Married? Relationship

Keiko, the star of 13 Reasons Why, is a happily married woman. On December 19, 2005, she and Shin Kawasaki exchanged wedding bands in a helicopter above Las Vegas, Nevada.

How did Keiko Agena start her Professional Career?

Since 1993, Keiko has been active in the film industry. Her acting début was in the television movie Renegade. After that, she appeared in several films and television programs, including 100%, Sister, Sister, ER, Beverly Hills, 90210, and Felicity.

In addition to Kelly Bishop, Sean Gunn, and Liz Torres, the 47-year-old actress came to prominence for her portrayal as Lane Kim on the 2000-2007 television series Gilmore Girls. She has also appeared in Hair Show, Private Valentine: Blonde & Dangerous, Labor Pains, Transformers: Dark of the Moon, Kim Possible, Scandal, Twisted, and Shameless.

Keiko Agena, the actress who portrays Rory Gilmore in the television series Gilmore Girls

In the Netflix television series 13 Reasons Why starring Amy Hargreaves, Justin Prentice, Ross Butler, Kate Walsh, and Brandon Flynn, Agena portrayed Pam Bradley from 2017 to 2018.

In addition, she has appeared in numerous television programs, including Gilmore Girls: A Year in the Life, NCIS: Los Angeles, Bajillion Dollar Propertie$, Dirty John, and The First.

Between 2019 and 2021, the Twisted actress appeared on Prodigal Son as Dr. Edrisa Tanaka, alongside Tom Payne, Lou Diamond Phillips, Frank Harts, and Halston Sage.
