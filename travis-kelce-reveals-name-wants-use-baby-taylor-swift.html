Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift have been dodging rumors of marriage and babies for months, with many fans wondering if and when the couple will take their relationship to the next level, given how fast they’ve moved since launching their relationship publicly last year.

Travis Kelce Wants To Name His Baby After Arnold Schwarzenegger

While Travis’ family has admitted the newfound fame that’s come with their relationship is exhausting (and a risk to their safety), Travis only fuelled the fan speculation when he recently admitted the name he wants to use if he starts a family with Taylor.

In the most recent installment of his podcast "New Heights," which he co-hosts with his older brother and former NFL center Jason, Travis delved into discussions about family planning while featuring Arnold Schwarzenegger as a guest.

The conversation initially revolved around Schwarzenegger's storied career, but veered into the realm of parenthood when the topic of Schwarzenegger's 1982 film "Conan the Barbarian" arose. This prompted Travis to reveal his thoughts on what he would name his future firstborn child.

"I might name my first kid Conan," he revealed with a joking tone. "I might."

Travis and Taylor have been tight-lipped on their plans for the future, but they’ve been largely inseparable despite Swift resuming her Eras tour following the Super Bowl. Travis has joined her on many tour stops, including Singapore and Australia, where a cute zoo date turned into criticism from PETA. More recently, the pair were seen taking a vacation in the Bahamas while Taylor enjoys an Easter break from her record-making tour.

Some Fans Think Taylor Will Announce A Pregnancy This Year

While it would be surprising for Taylor to announce a pregnancy any time soon, given all she’s got going on career-wise, there have been previous rumors that she and Travis have babies on the brain. At the start of this year, some fans were convinced Taylor will announce a pregnancy before the end of 2024, though nothing has come so far.

In an interview with Closer Magazine at the time, public relations expert Sally Morgan offered her insights and forecasts for the couple in the upcoming year. Morgan suggested that starting a family and possibly getting married are likely top priorities for them.
