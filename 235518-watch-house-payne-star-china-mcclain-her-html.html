Watch 'House of Payne' Star China McClain &amp; Her Only Brother Gabriel Dance Together on TikTok

 Posted on June 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  439 words  |  Larita Shotwell

“House of Payne” star China Anne McClain and her brother, Gabriel, treated her over 6.5 million Instagram followers with a hilarious video of them dancing.

China Anne McClain and her brother, Gabriel, are hilarious in a new TikTok video. To record the short clip, the siblings used the app's green-screen feature to add the photo of a creepy, abandoned hallway in the background.

At the bottom of the video, they wrote, “let your sibling pick your punishment or walk the hallway.” When the clip started, both China and Gabriel appeared looking at the eerie hall and then at the camera.

China McClain on November 14, 2010 in Hollywood, California | Photo: Getty Images

GABRIEL AND CHINA MCCLAIN’S TIKTOK VIDEO

After that, the siblings started showing off their best dance moves while slowly walking back toward the hallway to the song's beat.

Reaching the end of the video, Gabriel and China turned around and she jumped on his back before they stormed off. What made the video even funnier was that China held up a peace sign as they went deeper into the hallway.

Apart from Gabriel, who worked next to Adam Sandler, Kevin James, and Julie Bowen in Netflix’s 2020 film “Hubie Halloween,” China has two sisters, Sierra and Lauryn.

Every member of the McClain family is into the entertainment industry as Sierra is known for portraying Nessa Parker in “Empire,” and Lauryn appeared on “Step Up: High Water.”

[Cameron Boyce] was her best, closest friend.

CHINA MCCLAIN’S GLOW-UP

Most people probably remember China for her successful run in Disney Channel’s “ANT Farm” or the “Descendants” franchise. Well, the cute little girl grew up into a beautiful, talented actress and singer.

Apart from starring in “House of Payne” for years, China is also portraying Jennifer Pierce, a.k.a. Lightning, in the CW superhero show “Black Lightning.”

If that wasn’t enough to prove just how a talented actress China is, she has taken home an Image Award and two NAMIC Vision Awards. It is important to point out that she is only 22 years old.

While the actress has had a thriving career in the entertainment industry, she has faced some tough challenges, too, including grieving the death of Cameron Boyce, who died on July 6, 2019, after suffering from a seizure.

China and Cameron worked together on “Descendants” and were very close friends. In fact, she revealed in an Instagram video shared shortly after his death that he was her best, closest friend.

At the moment, China is very much focused on her career, being one of the main stars in “House of Payne” and “Black Lightning,” two highly-acclaimed shows.
