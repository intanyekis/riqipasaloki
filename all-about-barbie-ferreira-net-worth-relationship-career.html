Barbie Ferreira was born on December 14, 1996, in Queens, New York City. She is a plus-size model, actress, and social media influencer. She is also well-known for her role in the television show “Euphoria.”

Barbie will play Kat Hernandez in the show, which follows a group of high school teenagers as they deal with concerns like social networking, friendship, sex, and drugs. The show’s second season premiered in July 2019. Euphoria was created by Tmira Yardeni, Ron Leshem, and Daphna Levin.

How did Barbie Ferreira start her Professional Career?

Barbie started her self-titled YouTube account in high school, posting cosmetic tutorials. She then directed Dounia’s “So Cool” (American Musical Artist) music video.

In 2016, her Aerie Campaign photographs and interview became popular on the internet. She formerly worked in a mall in New Jersey before moving back to New York City to seek a modeling career.

Barbie later began her career in the fashion industry as a Plus-size model, and her first modeling opportunity was with the brand “American Apparel.” She has also worked with several Big Brands over her career.

In 2016, she was named one of Time’s “30 Most Influential Teens,” and she starred in the ten-episode Vice series “How to Behave,” about etiquette.

She then starred in Teen Vogue’s web series “Body Party,” for which she won a Webby Award for Best Web Personality/Host for How to Behave. In 2018, she made her television debut as Ella in “Divorce.”
