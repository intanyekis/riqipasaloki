Who Are Steven Tylers Children? The Aerosmith Singer Is a Father of 4 Including Actress Liv Tyler

 Posted on July 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  598 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Aerosmith's lead singer Steven Tyler has had one of the longest and most successful music careers in history, and his four children have always supported him. However, he has battled demons along the way.

Steven Tyler and his legendary rock band Aerosmith made headlines on May 24, 2022, after canceling the June and July shows of their Las Vegas residency so that Steven could enter rehab.

The band's Twitter statement read that although Steven had been sober for years, he relapsed after taking pain medication following his foot surgery. Steven is determined to get better and voluntarily entered a treatment program. Aerosmith will resume its shows in September 2022.

Steven Tyler on August 4, 2012 in Oakland, California | Source: Getty Images

WHO ARE STEVEN TYLER'S CHILDREN?

It must be hard for Steven to relapse at 74, but he is probably getting his children's support. Steven's eldest kid is Liv Tyler, an actress who shot to stardom after working on "Armageddon" and the "Lord of the Rings" film trilogy.

Born in 1977, Liv didn't know Steven was her father growing up. Her mother, model Bebe Buell (more on her later), had just ended her relationship with "Hello It's Me" singer Todd Rundgren when Liv was born. Naturally, Liv assumed Todd was her dad.

Chelsea Tyler, Mia Tyler, Steven Tyler, Liv Tyler and Taj Tyler on May 02, 2016 in New York | Source: Getty Images

Liv didn't know why, but she always felt a solid connection to Steven as a little girl. When she was ten, she noticed she had too many similarities to the Aerosmith frontman and asked her mom about it, who came clean about her dad's identity.

Luckily, Liv grew up with both Todd and Steven as father figures. She is raising three children of her own – one with Spacehog singer Royston Langdon and two with Motley Fool founder David Gardner.

Steven's second daughter is Mia Tyler, a self-described visual artist. The proud mother of one has done everything from modeling and acting, and she has also appeared on reality TV shows.

Chelsea Tyler is Steven's youngest daughter. She was born in 1989 and became a musician. Chelsea and her husband, Jon Foster, have a son and are members of the electronic-soul duo Kane Holler.

Steven's only son is Taj, born in 1991. Unlike his sisters, Taj prefers to stay away from the limelight and is not involved in the entertainment industry.

Steven is in a relationship with Aimee Ann Preston, 40 years his junior.

WHO ARE THE MOTHERS OF STEVEN'S CHILDREN?

Steven has four children with three different women. Liv's mother is Bebe Buell, who was also romantically involved with Mick Jagger, Iggy Pop, Elvis Costello, and a few other music stars.

Model Cyrinda Foxe is Mia's mom and Steven's first wife. They were married between 1978 and 1987, but she, unfortunately, died of cancer in 2002.

Chelsea and Taj's mother is designer Teresa Barrick, Steven's second wife. The former couple got married in 1988 and divorced in 2006.

WHO IS STEVEN DATING NOW?

Nowadays, Steven is in a relationship with Aimee Ann Preston, who is 40 years his junior. She has had some high-profile jobs throughout the years.

Apart from working as a personal assistant in the Trump Organization, Aimee has worked for Donald Trump's ex-wife Marla Maples and David Beckham's wife, Victoria.

Steven hired Aimee as his assistant in 2012 and sparked dating rumors two years later. The couple made their first appearance as a couple in February 2016 and currently lives in Los Angeles. We wish Steven a speedy recovery!
