Who Is Marike De Klerk? FW De Klerk Wife or Partner

Marike De Klerk also known as the former first lady of South Africa is the ex-wife of FW De Klerk. Her husband served as the state president of State Africa from 1989-1994. 

The pair met at Potchefstroom University where Marike was studying for a degree in commerce. They tied the knot in 1959 and gave birth to three children named Jan, Willem, and Susan. 

However, it seems they were not fated to live together for the rest of their lives. They separated in 1996 because in 1994 her husband started having an affair with Elita Georgiades, the wife of Tony Georgiades.
