Who is Jakob Magnus?

Jakob Magnus is an American social media influencer and YouTuber. He is best known as the older brother of fellow YouTuber and pop singer, Gavin Magnus.

As of now, he has more than 106k subscribers on his self-titled YouTube channel.

He is the son of Dave Magnus (father) and Theresa Magnus (mother). He has two siblings named Gavin Magnus and Justin Magnus.
