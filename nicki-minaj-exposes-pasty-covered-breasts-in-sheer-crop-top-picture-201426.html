Va va voom, Nicki Minaj! The "Pills N Potions" rapper/singer, 31, bared her breasts in a sexy outfit at the Hot 97 Summer Jam at New Jersey's MetLife Stadium on Sunday evening, June 1. 

Minaj took the stage in a sheer metallic crop top, which offered up a view of her pasty-covered chest. She hid her nipples with silver star-shaped appliques. 

Known for her wild style, the one-time American Idol judge paired the revealing top with high-waisted black-and-white printed pants and black strappy heels. With her hair straightened and parted down the middle, Minaj shared photos of the look on her Instagram account. 

The "Young Money" diva reunited with fellow hip-hop superstars including Lil Wayne, Drake, and Birdman at the star-studded event, which also featured performances by 50 Cent, Nas, and Iggy Azalea.
