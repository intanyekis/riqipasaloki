Shotzi Had A Rough Night, Leaves Makeup Imprint (And Painful Expression) On Mat

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  211 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Shotzi really made an impression at WWE’s live event in Baltimore.

WWE hosted a live event at the CFG Bank Arena in Baltimore on Wednesday night. Shotzi was in tag team action on the show, and ended up hitting the mat so hard that her makeup left a imprint on the canvas.

“Rough night in Baltimore yesterday,” Shotzi wrote on Instagram. “Here is a perfect imprint of my makeup on the mat ? you can even see the painful expression on my face @wwe”

“Shotzi!!!! I can’t ??? it look like you said “ugh” ? it’s the eyelashes and eyebrows for me,” Bianca Belair, her tag team partner for the night, added. “she cute tho!”

Full results from the show (via WrestlingBodyslam.net) are below:

LA Knight defeats Jimmy Uso

Bianca Belair and Shotzi defeat Bayley and WWE Women’s Champion Iyo Sky

The Street Profits: Angelo Dawkins and Montez Ford defeat Joaquin Wilde and Cruz del Toro

Kevin Owens defeats Solo Sikoa

The OC: Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson defeated Pretty Deadly: Elton Prince and Kit Wilson

Bobby Lashley defeats Butch

AJ Styles and Randy Orton defeat Grayson Waller and Austin Theory

  • AJ Styles vs Austin Theory is declared No Contest when Grayson Waller interferes, setting up a tag team match.
