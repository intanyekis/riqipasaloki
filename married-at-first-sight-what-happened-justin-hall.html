Are Married At First Sight Season 15's Justin Hall And Alexis Williams Still Together?

During the 15th season of Married at First Sight, fans got to see Justin Hall and Alexis Williams walk down the aisle together. After that, the couple worked to try to figure out if they could build a happy life together.

On decision day, Hall and Williams initially decided to stay together. For anyone who thought that meant that Williams and Hall were getting their happily ever after, it didn't take long at all for that to be in question.

Unfortunately for anyone who hoped for Hall and Williams to make things work, they split up shortly after deciding to stay together. Fortunately for fans who want to know what went wrong for Williams and Hall, the former couple had a public post-relationship conversation.

Unfortunately for all involved, some Married at First Sight couples who've broken up seem to hate each other. On the other hand, some MAFS duos have remained on good terms even though they are no longer together.

Since their relationship ended, Williams and Hall have stayed friendly with each other. That fact allowed Hall and Williams to come together to discuss their MAFS experiences with each other in a public venue

As of June 2024, Williams and Hall's public conversation has mostly flown under the radar among the Married at First Sight fan community. The video, which features a compelling conversation about what it is like to star in MAFS, only had roughly 6,200 views.

In December 2023, Williams uploaded a video on her YouTube channel that included a 55-minute conversation between her and Hall. During that conversation, Hall discussed what he thought made their relationship not work.

In response, Hall explained why he wished he'd been more perceptive during the bachelor party too. "Yup. Because, you know, if you read the room, you know, you can sort of sense, almost what's about to happen. You know, and a lot of times, you can, kind of, strategize to de-escalate the situation before it happens."

Justin Hall Received A Major Health Update After Married At First Sight

During Justin Hall and Alexis Williams' relationship post-mortem video, he referenced his emotions being out of his control several times. That may have seemed odd for some Married at First Sight fans, but he has a really good explanation for that.

In October 2023, Monsters and Critics published an article about a major health update Hall shared on Instagram. On the social media network, Hall announced that he'd been diagnosed with Klinefelter syndrome.

According to the Mayo Clinic, Klinefelter syndrome can cause an increased risk of anxiety, depression, being tall, and many more symptoms. People with Klinefelter Syndrome can also suffer from social, emotional, and behavioral problems, such as low self-esteem, emotional immaturity, and impulsiveness.

The Mayo Clinic's article about Klinefelter syndrome explains what causes the genetic disorder. As that article explains, almost all men are born with 46 chromosomes, including XY chromosomes. However, the most common form of Klinefelter syndrome, which Justin Hall has, is caused by men being born was an XXY sex chromosome.

During Hall and Williams' aforementioned video, Hall briefly spoke about how his life improved after his diagnosis. After learning he was born with Klinefelter Syndrome, he began receiving treatment that changed his life for the better.

"A lot of the unregulated emotions you guys saw during the show was due to that. Actually, one of the number one side effects of it is unregulated emotions. So, now I am on treatment... I love it. It's probably the best thing that ever happened to me."

Hall continued, "That's why I appreciate my time on MAFS because I don't think, you know, this red flag would have came, and it would have raised the red flags with my current partner to go get tested."

Justin Hall Got A New Girlfriend After His Breakup With Alexis Williams

While Justin Hall was discussing being diagnosed with Klinefelter Syndrome, he mentioned having a new partner. As Reality TV World revealed in September 2023, Hall and the new woman in his life went Instagram official.

On Instagram, Hall shared photos of himself and his blonde girlfriend attending a wedding together. Hall also shared photos of himself and his girlfriend celebrating her birthday.

While talking to Williams during their video, Hall explained that his girlfriend helped him get diagnosed with Klinefelter Syndrome. As Hall explained, his girlfriend's interest in holistic medicine inspired her to ask him if he'd ever had his testosterone levels tested. After initially being defensive, Hall got tested and the diagnosis soon followed.
