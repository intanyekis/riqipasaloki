Blazing Visuals graphic design extraordinaire Matt Cornier dies at age 32

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  390 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Toms River, NJ – Toms River, New Jersey, is grieving the untimely passing of a creative luminary, Matt Cornier, a talented graphic artist whose vibrant spirit touched the lives of many. Cornier, known for his infectious smile and creative prowess, departed on Wednesday, December 6, 2023, leaving behind a devastated circle of family and friends.

Confirmation of his passing reverberated through social media, with heartfelt tributes acknowledging his magnetic personality. One post, in particular, encapsulated the sentiment, praising Cornier for his ability to light up any room with his infectious smile.

Cornier’s journey into the world of design commenced with academic excellence, culminating in a Bachelor of Arts degree in Graphic Design from Kean College-Ocean County College. His passion for creativity led him to establish the renowned clothing line, Syren, as an outlet for his artistic expression.

Employed as a Design Artist at Blazing Visuals in Point Pleasant, Cornier’s dedication and talent shone through in every project. His life philosophy, encapsulated by the mantra “Live to Die Happy,” echoed his commitment to embracing life’s moments.

Hailing from Toms River, Cornier’s roots were deeply embedded in the community. His high school years at Toms River High School East were marked by active involvement in lacrosse and significant contributions to the school’s football team, which achieved an undefeated record during his tenure. Additionally, his achievements extended to earning the esteemed rank of Eagle Scout with Boy Scout Troop 1, highlighting his dedication and pride in his accomplishments.

Danny DiAngelo posted on Facebook,

“It’s so crazy I’m actually writing this. Matt Cornier you are truly going to be missed. You had the most infectious smile and you could light up the darkest room. I’m going to miss all the laughs we have shared over the years. Rest in peace brother”

Survived by his fiancée, Danielle Bethencourt, and a loving family, including his parents, siblings, grandparents, and extended relatives, Cornier’s presence touched the lives of many.

A Funeral Mass honoring Matt Cornier’s life is scheduled for Monday, December 11, 2023, at 11:00 am at St. Justin’s Roman Catholic Church, located at 975 Fischer Boulevard, Toms River.

His passing leaves a profound void in the hearts of those who knew him, yet his artistic legacy and vibrant spirit will endure in the memories of family, friends, and a community united in mourning his loss.
